Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time

Walkthrough

Here is a walkthrough video of getting past the guards in the Castle Courtyard.

Hyrule Castle Courtyard

The castle courtyard is full of guards you need to sneak past. This is not that difficult. The path the guards walk are all the same and you just need to watch how they walk their paths. They don't have a very close radar to pick up, so you can all most just follow them as they are moving and then stopping. The first has one guard that is walking around a square hedge. He will stop at each of the corners. You can wait till he gets to the other side and easily walk past.

The 2nd spot has two water fountains with two guards. One is walking around the first fountain and the other around the second fountain. Once they are both on the back side you can easily run past them.

The 3rd spot has one guard walking in a square around a trellis that you can cross from the top if you wish. There are also (Rupees) around the bottom that you can collect if you wish to be risky. Once the guard is on the back side or facing the back side you just run past to the next area.

The 4th spot has two guards walking around a statue. You will need to wait for both guards to pass around the front before coming out. You can follow the second guard till he makes his turn at the other end.

The 5th spot is the final area you will have sneak past. The screen shifts to moving north and south. There are two guards here as well, once they are on one side just go to the opposite side and run to the end.

Hyrule Castle Main Courtyard

You have made it to the main Courtyard that Princes Zelda is at, Zelda is shocked to see you come up behind her. Zelda then realizes that you are from the forest with a fairy. Zelda ask if you have the (Spiritual Stone of the Forest). Zelda is excited to hear that you have the stone.

Zelda then tells Link about a dream she had of a figure from the forest with the green stone. She then introduces herself as Zelda Princess of Hyrule. Zelda then begins to tell the secret of the Sacred Realm that has been passed down by the Royal Family of Hyrule. The three goddesses hid the Triforce containing the power of the gods somewhere in Hyrule. The power to grant the wish of the one who holds the Triforce in his hands. If someone with a righteous heart makes a wish, it will lead Hyrule to a golden age of prosperity... If someone with an evil mind has his wish granted, the world will be consumed by evil... That is what has been told... So the ancient Sages built the Temple of Time to protect the Triforce from evil ones.

Entering the Temple of Time is the entrance through wich you can enter the Sacred Realm from our world. The entrance is sealed with a stone wall called the Door of Time. Only way to open the door is to collect the three Spiritual Stones and the treasure that the Royal Family keeps the (Ocarina of Time).

The dark clouds from Zeldas dream she believes they symbolize that man in the window. Ganodorf the leader of the Gerudos. Zelda told her father of the dream and he didn't believe it was a phrophecy.

Zelda believes they are the only ones that can protect Hyrule. Now Link needs to get the other two Spiritual Stones. Zelda then gives you a letter that will be helpful.

Impa of the Sheikahs is waiting for you at the exit to lead you out of the castle. Impa first teaches Link the ancient meldoy passed down by the the Royal Family. Link has learned his first song (Zelda's Lullaby). Impa then leads Link out of the castle. She informs Link of Death Mountain home of the Gorons. They hold the (Spiritual Stone of Fire). Your next stop is Kakariko Village that is at the foot of Death Mountain. Back on Hyrule Field you can advance to Kakariko Village or explore Hyrule Field. Lon Lon Ranch is south of the castle follow the path to reach it. You can go back into the Castle Market, or return to the Kokirk Forest. This guide will have you go back to the Castle Market to complete a few Side Quest then move to Lon Lon Ranch then back to Kokirk Forest then advance to Kakariko Village. This will give you a few more (Gold Skultulas), (Heart Containers) and a song that will be required when you get to Death Mountain.
