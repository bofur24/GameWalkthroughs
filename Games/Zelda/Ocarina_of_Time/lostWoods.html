Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time

Walkthrough

Lost Woods

Return to Kokiri Forest cross the bridge then head to the north side and climb the vines on the wall. Circle around to the entrance of the Lost Woods. The Lost Woods are a maze, so if you go the wrong direction, it sends you back outside to the Kokiri Forest and start over again. The key to going the corret direction, is to listen to the sound of the music playing in the area as you try to enter a new area. Here is a map to help if you get lost.

Now head to the east first. THis next area has two different sid quests to complete. The first one is you need to pull out our (Fairy Slingshot) and aim at the bullseye. Hit the center three times to get 100s and be rewarded with another Deku Sedds Bullet Bag that will now hold 50 bullets.

Climb down the ladder to get to the area below. Run to the stump and stand on it then two Skull Kids show up with flutes. This quest is Encore, Encore! to gain another (Piece of Heart). Pull out your (Fairy Ocarina). This game is all about following along as they play their notes. The first round is five notes and you will be rewared with a (Green Ruppee). The second round is six notes and you will be rewared with a (Blue Ruppee). The final round is eight notes and you will be rewared with the (Piece of Heart). Now climb back up the ladder and head to the north.

The next area you are gretted by the Giant Owl. He will talk to Link about getting through the Lost Woods. He says to listen to the music as you try to access each area. Once you make it past the Lost Woods you will come upon the Sacred Forest Meadow. Once the Giant Owl flies away enter the tunnel he was sitting on to the east.

Next area head north then north again then west finally to the north to make it to the Sacred Forest Meadow. Link sees a gate that is closed; to open this gate Link must defeat (Wolfos). Once the gate opens you enter in to a small maze.

There are (Mad Scrubs) you need to deal with. Just like the (Deku Scrubs) you just need to pull out your (Deku Shield) and knock back their nuts they fire at you. They will run around trying to get back in their hole. You will need to attack them as they run arround. At the end of the maze there is a ladder you can climb to be on top of the maze. There is a hole you can fall in to find a Fairy Fountain. Grab a fairy to heal all your harts. If you have an empty bottle you can capture one to use later if you die. The fairy will fill all your hart back for you. Leave the fountain then head towards the north stairs and ascend them. There are two more (Mad Scrubs) in your way. Finish them off then ascend the final stairs to reach your desired location.
