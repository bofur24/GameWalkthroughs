Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time

Walkthrough

Intro

This walkthrough was played on the Nintendo Gamecube. The original game was released on Nintendo 64 back in 1998, this game was released in 2003. When you get past the title screen you will create your own save file. You can name yourself whatever you like within the number of characters allowed.

The game starts with the (Great Deku Tree) talking of a young boy without a fairy. Then transitions to a drawbridge coming down from a castle where a lady and young lady come running out of the castle on horseback. As they run past the boy, he turns to see another dark figure stare him down. You see the boy in shock from the dark stare from the man.

Back to he (Great Deku Tree) speaking to a fairy of the name of (Navi). The (Great Deku Tree) speaks to (Navi) of the evil descending upon the realm. The (Great Deku Trees') power is nothing compared to this evil. It is time for the boy without a fairy to begin his journey. The (Great Deku Tree) instructs (Navi) to find the boy and guide him to the (Great Deku Tree).

Kokiri Forest

(Navi) flies off to find (Link), (Navi) finds (Link) asleep in his home. (Navi) then wakes (Link) to inform him that the (Great Deku Tree) has summed him and (Navi) the fairy will be your new partner. As (Link) steps out of his house he is greeted by Saria.

She is excited that a fairy has come to (Link)s aid. Saria instructs (Link) to not let the (Great Deku Tree) wait to see you. Head to the east towards the (Great Deku Tree). Cross the small amount of water and then you are stopped by a Kokiri boy. He states that you are not a real man with out a fairy, but then sees that (Link) has one indeed. Mido is shocked by the news that you are going to see the (Great Deku Tree). He refuses to let you by without being properly equipped. You need to find a sword and shield. Your new task is to find a Sword and Shield.

When you turn around (Navi) may have some information for you. Press the UP C BUTTON. This information is a way to remind you what your next task is. (Navi) informs you that the (Great Deku Tree) has summoned you. Head back west to the hill next to your tree house. This area is called the Forest Training Center. This area has many signs that will teach you different techniques of combat so when you get your sword it can be good to try them out. At the top you will come to a small crawl space you need to enter. This small maze is where you will find your sword. The major danger is the large bolder rolling around the area. When you come to the Large Chest open it to receive the (Kokiri Sword). Enter your equipment screen and equip the (Kokiri Sword).

Now that you have the (Kokiri Sword) read the signs and practice your sword play. Once you leave the Forest Training Center to the west is the house of the Know-it-All Brothers. They will teach you about different game elements. The first one you come to will teach you about the Yellow Icons or the C Buttons and about the Green Icon, the Action Icon or the A Button. The next borther will teach you how to use C Icons. The last bother has information about the Map and Items. Once you have talked to all three leave the house and move to the north house. This is Midos house, as you enter his home you see four chest. Each chest has items that you will learn about as you open them. The two on the farside of the house are (Blue Rupees), the closer chest is a (Recovery Heart) and a (Green Rupee). Out side of Midos house you will see a Kokiri kid trying to pick up a rock. Pick them up and throw them to receive some rupees.

Head to the east next to your house to visit Saria's house. There is a Kokiri kid outiside cutting the grass for Mido. Cut the grass your self to gain some rupees. When you enter Saria's house you will find (Recovery Hearts). On the roof of Saria's house, you can walk along the bridge to reach another young lady hanging around and you can receive a (Blue Rupee).

The house next to Saria's is the Twins' house. One of the twins are home and will inform (Link) of the different types of Rupees. The last building you need to enter is the Kokiri Shop. Before entering you are called upon by the other twin on the roof. She teaches (Link) how to use his fairy partner (Navi) to talk to her at a distance.

Enter the Kokiri Shop to see the items for sale. You only need to buy the (Deku Shield) for 40 Rupees. If you don't have enough, you can go cut grass, throw rocks, break jars in the homes and run in the tall grass to gain the Rupees you need.

Now return to Mido to show him you are now equipped and ready to go see the (Great Deku Tree).

Once you arive at the (Great Deku Tree) he ask (Link) to listen. Thy slumber these past moons must have been restless, and full of nightmares. As the servants of evil gain strength, a vile climate pervades the land and causes nightmares to those sensitive to it. Verily, thou has felt it. (Link) The time has come to test thy courage, I have been cursed, I need you to break the curse with your wisdom and courage. Then enter, brave (Link), and thou too (Navi).
