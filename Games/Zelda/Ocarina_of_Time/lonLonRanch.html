Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time

Walkthrough

Lon Lon Ranch (Day)

Inside of Lon Lon Ranch enter the door to the west first. Inside you will find a disgruntled man name Ingo. He is not happy that Talon runs the ranch when he is lazy, and Ingo does all the work.

Exit the stables then enter the door on the east side. You find Talon asleep with all his Cuccos running around. You wake him, he notices who you are and thanks you for what you did the other day. Talon asks if you are up for a little game. The three Cuccos here are Super Cuccos that he is going to throw in to the gaggle of normal Cuccos. Your task is to find the three super Cuccos before time runs out. Link has 30 seconds, and the cost is 10 Rupees to play. The best strategy is to turn and watch the shadow of the two behind him fly off and grab them as quickly as you can when they land. The last one is mized in the gaggle in front of Talon. If you havea keen eye, you can see the one that behaves differently in speed. If not just grab as many as you can till, he says you found the correct one. If you fail, you can try again for only 5 Rupees. After you complete his game he jokes about you marrying Malon. Your reward is a bottle of (Lon Lon Milk). After the game you can head upstairs to find some jars to break if you need some more Rupees.

Exit this room to be back outside, then go south to the main area of the Ranch. Turn to your east to the loan tree. Do a roll attck in to it to expose another (Gold Skulltula). 6/100

Now head to the center of the fenced area where you will find Malon singing a song. Malon asks if you got to see the Princess and thanks you for waking her dad. Malon introduces you to her horse Epona. Epona is afraid of you at the moment. Speak with Malon one more time and she talks about a song her mother composed. Pull out your (Fairy Ocarina) to learn her song.

You have now learned Epona's Song! Epona is now friendly towards you and starts pushing you. Now you can explore more of the ranch. Under the small stable you can find some more jars to break. Leave the fenced area to the west and head to the southwest corner to a door next to the outsidewall.

In this room is the Lon Lon Luck (Piece of Heart). You need to move the crates around to access a small hole in the back of the area to crawl in.

Pick up the (Piece of Heart). You now have 1/2 of the heart to form a full (Heart Container). That is all there is to do during the day. You need to leave and return when its nightfall.

Lon Lon Ranch (Night)

Enter back into Lon Lon Ranch, the door on the west is open with Ingo still being angry about his situation. The door on th east side is locked. As you head towards the main area. Look up above the locked door on the upstairs window. There is another (Gold Skulltula). You can't get this one as of right now, you will have to return once you get the (Boomerang). Following the outside wall to the west you will find another (Gold Skulltula) on the high wall. Once again you can't reach this one without the (Boomerang). As you are walking around you will be atacked from above by (Guay). Keep circling around to the back side of the small fenced in area where the small stable. There is a (Gold Skulltula) you can collect here. That is all you can do here untill you come back with the (Boomerange)
