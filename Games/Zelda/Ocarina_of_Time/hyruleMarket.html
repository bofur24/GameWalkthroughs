Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time

Walkthrough

Hyrule Market (Day)

Once you cross the drawbridge enter the door on your right. This room is filled with jars that you can break to gain (Rupees). This is one of the best areas to gain (Rupees) when you are in need. You can beak them all then leave and come back in and do it again. There is a (Gold Skulltula) in here as well. The two crates at the back of the room you will need to roll into the one near the steps to expose the (Gold Skulltula). 5/100 Leave this room and run to the next area.

The main market area is bustling with people. To the west is access to the Back Alley. There is not much going on in the back alley. The first door you come to is the Treasure Chest Contest that is only open during the night. You can keep going around the back ally and find locked doors and empty houses. Once you have gone thourgh all the back ally you will be on the north side of the Market. Head back south to the door on the west side next to the stairs to enter the Bombchu Bowling Alley. This side game is not open right now.

You will have to comeback later after advancing in the main quest. Exit this room then head to the north door with a target and arrow on the wall. This is the Shooting Gallery. This game is 20 Rupees per play. There are 10 targets. You have 15 chances to hit the 10 targets!

  • Hit 10 targets - Perfect Prize (Deku Seed Bullet bag)
  • Hit 8 targets or more - Free Retry
  • Hit less than 8 - Game Over
When your done exit this area.

Here is a video guide to get a perfect score.

Now head to the east to the next shop the Happy Mask Shop. It is not running right now but you can read the rules required to sale mask. When the shop opens you will have the mask you want loaned to you then you will find someone that will want to buy it from you. Return to the mask shop and pay the amount back and you keep the profit you sold it for. Exit this shop and outside speak with the Woman in the blue dress. she will have a side quest when you come back at night. She will inform you that her dog can run faster than any of the other mutts around here.

Your next stop is the Potion Shop. Many of the items in this shop you can't buy without having a container to put it in too. So you can just check the prices and see if they are worth coming back for later. Exit the shop and enter the last shop on the west side. This is the Bazaar. This shop sales many of the common items you use or have obtained. The main item you need to buy here is the (Hylian Shield) for 80 (Rupees).

If you don't have the (Rupees) go to the door next to the drawbridge and break the jars for (Rupees). After buying the (Hylian Shield) you should exit the shop then exit this area to the north to the Hyrule Castle area to let time pass to night.

Hyrule Market (Night)

Enter the market at night and you will see dogs running all around the area. Just run past them all head to the Back Alley south side. The first door to your left is the Treasure Chest Contest that was closed during the day. Now it is open, but no one is in the shop. Exit and enter the next door to speak withe Woman in the blue dress again. She informs you that she can't find her puppy anywhere. This is the One Dog Night (Heart piece) side quest.

Return to the Main area of the Market. Her dog is the white dog near the Bazaar. It is the fatest dog of all them running around. Walk next to the dog and it will start following you. Head back to the womans house and show her the dog. If you got the correct dog she will reward you with a (Piece of Heart). This is only a 1/4 of a heart as you complete more side quest you will get more to complete a heart to then increase your hearts. Now that you have completed her quest you are finished in the Hyrule Market for now. You will need to go back to Hyrule Castle area to have time pass to be able to leave the Market since the dawbridge is up.
