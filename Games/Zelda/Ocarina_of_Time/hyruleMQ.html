Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time

Walkthrough

Hyrule Field

(Link) has now entered Hyrule Field. Once you turn the corner you are face with a Giant Owl. This owl will guide you on your quest of things to come and where to go. This owl explains about the map in your sub screens. Hyrule Castle is on the far north side of the area. Head straight north to rech the drawbridge to enter Hyrule Castle Market. You may not make it there in time due to the passing of time in Hyrule Field. Once it gets dark the drawbridge will rise and you can't enter untill daytime. While you wait for daytime enemies will spawn and attack you. Two () will spawn at a time. Take them out as you please and gain (Rupees) and (Recovery Hearts) if needed. When the sun rises go across the drawbridge.

Hyrule Castle Market

Hyrule Castle Market is the first stop before heading to Hyrule Castle. There are a few thing you can do here, but we are just going on with the main quest for now. On the north side of the fountain you see a young girl humming a song. Speak to her and she will tell you her name is Malon. She and her dad are the owners of the Lon Lon Ranch. Her father went to the castle to deliver some milk and had not come back. After speaking to Malon head north to Hyrule Castle.

Hyrule Castle

Return to the Kokiri Forest and meet up with Mido blocking your path. He is not happy to hear about the (Great Deku Tree) passing, he will then let you pass. Now is the time to leave the Kokiri Forest. Head all the way to the west side of the area to leave. The kid blocking the path will now allow you to enter. As you start to run across the bridge (Link) hears Saria's voice. She knew that (Link) would leave the forest someday becasue he is different from Saria and her friends. But that's ok, because we'll be friends forever. She gives (Link) the (Fairy Ocarina) as a show of them being true friends. When you play my (Fairy Ocarina), I hope you will think of me and come back to the forest to visi. (Link) then runs off.
