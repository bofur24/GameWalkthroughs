Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time

Walkthrough

Hyrule Field

Link has now entered Hyrule Field. Once you turn the corner you are face with a Giant Owl. This owl will guide you on your quest of things to come and where to go. This owl explains about the map in your sub screens. Hyrule Castle is on the far north side of the area. Head straight north to reach the drawbridge to enter Hyrule Castle Market. You may not make it there in time due to the passing of time in Hyrule Field. Once it gets dark the drawbridge will rise and you can't enter untill daytime. While you wait for daytime enemies will spawn and attack you. Two () will spawn at a time. Take them out as you please and gain (Rupees) and (Recovery Hearts) if needed. When the sun rises go across the drawbridge.

Hyrule Castle Market

Hyrule Castle Market is the first stop before heading to Hyrule Castle. There are a few things you can do here, but we are just going on with the main quest for now. On the north side of the fountain you see a young girl humming a song. Speak to her and she will tell you her name is Malon. She and her dad are the owners of the Lon Lon Ranch. Her father went to the castle to deliver some milk and had not come back. After speaking to Malon head north to Hyrule Castle.

Hyrule Castle

As you move towards the castle you are greated by the Giant Owl again. He informs you that the princess is inside the castle, but to be careful not to get caught by the guards. He will explain the flow of time in this area is normal and when in towns time stands still. So, if you want to enter an area at night you need to be in one of these areas to have time advance to that point then enter the town or area. The tree the owl was sitting on has a (Gold Skulltula) in it. Do a roll attack to knock the (Gold Skulltula) out of the tree. 4/100

After collecting your Token head back to the wall on the east side and climb the vines to the top of the hill. Run across this area and jump back down to the path. Keep heading to the east to a bolder, you can't do anything as of now with this bolder, but when you have access to bombs you can come back and blow it up to reach a Great Fairy. Following the path back north you will see two guards standing and another on the hill to your west. You can walk up the hill just west off the path between these guards with out being seen. Now follow the path in the grass all the way to the far west side to some stones on the side of the wall to climb over the castle wall. Keep heading to the west on this hill side then jump down to the moat.

Jump in the moast and head to the east to the corner where it then turns north. Climb out at this point then head north. You will find Malons father sleeping on the gournd. Getting past him you need to find a way to wake him. Now that you have found Malons father you need to return to her. Walk back south then west to the guards at the gate. They will then throw you out. Walk back to the vine you climbed up to see that Malon is waiting for you. Talk to her and she will give you an egg she has been incubating. The (Weird Egg) is needed to wake her father.

You will need to let time pass for 24 hours for the egg to hatch. Now head back the same way you did to get back to her father. Now you need to wait for the egg to hatch. Once it hatches you will now have a chicken you can use to wake her father. He wakes up tells you his name is Talon the owner of Lon Lon Ranch. He then runs off to go find Malon. Now that the path is clear you can move the Lon Lon crates to then jump to the small water flowing out of the wall to get into the castle.
