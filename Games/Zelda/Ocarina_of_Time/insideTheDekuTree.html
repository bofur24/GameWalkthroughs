Walkthrough

Inside the Deku Tree

Here is a walkthrough video of the dungeon Inside the Deku Tree or you can follow along the guide below.

1st Floor Main Room

After entering the Deku Tree there will be a big spider web in the center of the room that you can't break at this time. There are three enemies (Deku Baba) that you need to deal with, that may drop a new item (Deku Nut). When you are ready to move on you can climb the ladder or the vines to access the second floor.

2nd Floor Main Room

As you circle around the outside you will come to some vines you can climb, but there are some (Skullwalltula) blocking the path. You will have to comeback to deal with them after advancing further on the second floor. Between the vines you will find a large chest, open to receive the (Dungeon Map). Keep moving around the outside jumping the gap to a door.

2nd Floor 1st Room

Once you enter this door you will come face to face with a (Deku Scrub) that will popout out of the ground and shoot a nut at you. Pull out your shield and block it to make it bounce back at him. You will not need to kill him as he tries to run away. Just block him from reentering his hole. He will give up and ask you to spare him if he gives you a clue. He will give you a tip when jumping off high cliffs, to hold forward to roll on the ground when you land and won't get hurt from the fall. There are no guarantees it will work, if the cliff is really high. Once he runs off, he leaves a (Recovery Heart) for you. Grab it if you may have lost any. Enter the next door.

2nd Floor 2nd Room

The next room you see a floating platform, as you jump to it and land it will begin to fall, hurry and jump to the other side. If you don't make it, that is ok you can climb the vines on the wall to get up. Once you have made it to the other side there is a large chest in the middle of the room. Open this chest to receive the (Fairy Slingshot). Equip the (Fairy Slingshot) to one of your (C buttons) it will be used to get out of this room. First look around for some more vines to climb higher in this area. You will find a small chest with a (recovery heart).

Turn and look back to the door you came in and (Navi) will guide you to a ladder above that area. Target the ladder, then pull your (Fairy Slingshot) out and shoot to knock the ladder down to access the ladder. Jump down if you need to replenish anything cut the small, withered grass. You may get some (Deku Seeds). Now climb the ladder back out the door to the first room, then back out to the main room.

2nd Floor Main Room

Circle back to the vines with the (Skullwalltula), now you can aim and shoot them with your (Fairy Slingshot). There are three in total to shoot. Two you can target and the last one you will have to manually aim at. Climb to the top and drop down on the third floor.

3rd Floor Main Room

As you circle this room you will encounter a (Big Skulltula) there are three around this room. Listen to (Navi) to see that its weak point is it back. Wait for the (Big Skulltula) to spin around and attack. You will need to hit it twice to kill. Keep circling around to the door and enter.

3rd Floor 1st Room

When you enter you see three platforms on the floor, an unlit torch to the left, a switch to the right and a lit brazier. When you step on the switch the platforms will rise, and a counter starts to tick down. Jump to the first then the second to reach the other side of the room. If you fall you will have to climb back up the vines on the entrance wall and start over. When you reach the other side there is a (Withered Baba) you can kill to get more (Deku Sticks). Open the large chest to receive the (Dungeon Compass).

Jump down to the center of the room then to the right you will face a (Big Skulltula). Defeat the (Big Skulltula) and the (Deku Baba) then climb the vine back to the start of the room. Step on the switch and make the jump to the open room to the left. Open the small chest to receive a (Recovery Heart). Behind the chest is a (Gold Skulltula). Kill the (Gold Skulltula) to collect a (Gold Skulltula Token). These are part of a side quest to kill 100. 1/100 Now jump back to the middle climb the vine and pull out a (Deku Stick) light it and light the unlit torch to open the door.

3rd Floor Main Room

Back in the third-floor main room you need to kill a (Big Skulltula) at the point were the (Recovery Heart) is closer to the platform. Jump from this point to land on the large spider web on the first floor. Doing so will send you to the first-floor basement.

1st Floor Basement Main Room

Once you fall though the web you will fall in the water below. Once you go out you can stand on a shallow area of the water. There are two ways you can go either climb the ledge with the switch or on the other side with the (Deku Baba). You should climb the side with the (Deku Baba) to kill it first to get it out of your way. On the wall where you fell in are vines and in the vines is a (Gold Skulltula). 2/100 Shoot it with your (Fairy Slingshot) then climb the vine to collect the (Gold Skulltula Token). Now head to the other side where the switch is and step on it. This will light the brazier and set the spider web on fire revealing a small chest. Open the chest to receive a (Recovery Heart). You will need to use this lit brazier, but first walk back to the switch and kill another (Gold Skulltula). 3/100 Once you kill the (Gold Skulltula), back up and make a running jump to collect the (Gold Skulltula Token). Now back to the lit brazier pull out a (Deku Stick) and set it on fire. Jump in the shallow water and cross to the other side to the spider web blocking the door to set the web on fire. Enter the door to the next room.

1st Floor Basement 1st Room

You will come face to face with another (Scrub) same as last time pull out your shield and block the nut he fires and bouce it back at him. Stop him from returning to his hole and he will teach you something helpful. He informs you to beat his bothers up ahead you need to punish them in the proper order. That order is 2..3..1 Twenty-three is number one! He will then run off leaving a recovery heart behind. Now to enter the next room, you will need to aim your (Fairy Slingshot) at the Sliver open eye above the door and shoot it. Enter the door to the next room.

1st Floor Basement 2nd Room

This room you will see water, a moving platform and a spike roller spinning. As you step close to the water (Navi) will inform you of diving in the water with the Action Button. Near the wall on the left is a switch that is underwater. Swim to the top of it and hold down the Action Button to swim down to press down the switch. The water will lower and a timer will start. Get back to the starting area and be quick to jump on the platform to get under the spinning spike roller.

Now on the other side you need to defeat a (Big Skulltula) blocking the path of the block you will need to move. When you get close to the block (Navi) will inform you of the actions you can do with the block. Now push the block all the way until you can't push anymore. Climb the block to access the next door. Enter the door.

1st Floor Basement 3rd Room

This room has one (Deku Baba) in the cneter and two (Withered Baba) around the outside. Take care of the (Deku Baba) this one will be in the way of lighting the two torches. The (Withered Baba) are only there if you need (Deku Sticks). Light your (Deku Stick) with the brazier then run and light the two torches to open the next door. Enter the door.

1st Floor Basement 4th Room

When you enter the room a (Big Skulltula) falls in front of your path; take care of him. There is one (Withered Baba) and hanging from the ceiling are three (Gohma Larva). They will drop on you when you walk under their shadow. You can avoid them as you stick to the outside of the room.

You will need to light your (Deku Sick) to burn the two spider webs blocking the path. One path is a dead end with a (Deku Baba) and a stone wall behind it. You will need to come back to this area when you have the bomb bag. The next spider web will give you access to the path you need to crawl through to get back to the first-floor basement main room.

1st Floor Basement Main Room

Back in the first-floor basement main room you need to push the block off the edge on to the shallow water. There are two (Deku Babas) if you want to take care of, but they may not be in your way. Crossing the water on the block you need to light your (Deku Stick) and jump back onto the block then swing it on the big spider web in the center of this room. You will fall to the bottom into a pool of water.

2nd Floor Basement Main Room

Climb out of the pool of water to be faced with 3 (Deku Scrubs), these are the three brother you were warned about. You will need to stop them in the order you were told to get the door open. The order is 2..3..1 Twenty-three is number one! Prepare your shield to bounce back the nuts fired at you. They will shoot at you from all sides. You need to bounce back the center one first, then head to the right and bounce his back then back to the far left to finish off the sequence.

2nd Floor Basement Boss Room

You enter the room, and a wall comes crashing down behind you trapping you in. The room is now dark all but a lone eye on the ceiling. You look up and see sees you and falls from the ceiling. (Parasitic Armored Arachinid GOHMA).

When you can lock on to her eye, when it glows red shoot her with the (Fairy Slingshot). She will then be stunned and your chance to swing your sword as many times as you can. She will then recuperate and retreat up the wall and on to the ceiling again. Pull out your (fairy Slingshot) and wiat for her eye to go read again and shoot. She will fall to the ground. Pull out your sword and keep hitting until you finish her off or she retreats to the wall again.

When she is defeated, she will drop a (Heart Container) that will give you one more heart. Step in the glowing circle to be sent back outside in front of the Deku Tree.
