Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time

Walkthrough

Inside the Deku Tree

Here is a walkthrough video of the dungeon Inside the Deku Tree or you can follow along the guide below.

1st Floor

After entering the Deku Tree there will be a big spider web in the center of the room that you can't break at this time. There are three enemies (Deku Baba) that you need to deal with, that may drop a new item (Deku Nut). When you are ready to move on you can climb the ladder or the vines to access the second floor.

2nd Floor Main Room

As you circle around the outside you will come to some vines you can climb, but there are some (Skullwalltula) blocking the path. You will have to comeback to deal with them after advancing further on the second floor. Between the vines you will find a large chest, open to receive the (Dungeon Map). Keep moving around the outside jumping the gap to a door.

2nd Floor 1st Room

Once you enter this door you will come to face with a (Scrub) that will popout of the ground and shoot a nut at you. Pull out your shield and block it to make it bounce back to him. You will not need to kill him as he tries to run away. Just block him from reentering his hole. He will give up and ask you to spare him if he give you a clue. He will give you a tip when jumping off high cliffs, to hold forward to roll on the ground when you land and won't get hurt from the fall. There are no guarantees it will work, if the clif is really, high. Once he runs off, he leaves a (Recovery Heart) for you. Grab it if you may have lost any. Enter the next door.

2nd Floor 2nd Room

The next room you see a floating platform, as you jump to it and land it will begin to fall, hurry and jump to the other side. If you don't make it, that is ok you can climb the vines on the wall to get up. Once you have made it to the other side ther is a large chest in the middle of the room. Open this chest to receive the Fairy Slingshot. Equip the Fairy Slingshot to one of your C buttons it will be used to get out of this room. First look around for some more vines to climb higher in this area. You will find a small chest with a recovery heart.

Turn and look back to the door you came in to and Navi will guide you to a ladder above that area. Target the ladder, then pull your Fairy Slingshot out and shoot to knock the ladder down to access the ladder. Jump down if you need to replenish anything cut the small, withered grass. You may get some Deku Seeds. Now climb the ladder back out the door to the first room, then back out to the main room.

2nd Floor Main Room

Circle back to the vines with the (Skullwalltula), now you can aim and shoot them with your Fairy Slingshot. There are three in total to shoot. Two you can target and the last one you will have to manually aim at. Climb to the top and drop down on the third floor.

3rd Floor Main Room

As you circle this room you will encounter a (Big Skulltula) there are three around this room. Listen to Navi to see that its weak point is it back. Wait for the (Big Skulltula) to spin around and attack. You will need to hit it twice to kill. Keep circling around to the door and enter.

3rd Floor 1st Room

When you enter you see three platforms on the floor, an unlit brazier to the left, a switch to the right and a lit brazier. When you step on the switch the platforms will rise, and a counter starts to tick down. Jump to the first then the second to reach the other side of the room. If you fall you will have to climb back up the vines on the entrance wall and start over. When you reach the other sie there is a (Withered Baba) you can kill to get more (Deku Sticks). Open the large chest to receive the (Dungeon Compass). Jump down to the center of the room then to the right you will face a (Big Skulltula). Defeat the (Big Skulltula) and the (Deku Baba) then climb the vine back to the start of the room. Step on the switch and make the jump to the open room to the left. Open the small chest to receive a (Recovery Heart). behind the chest is a (Gold Skulltula). Kill the (Gold Skulltula) to collect a (Gold Skulltula Token). These are part of a side quest to kill 100. 1/100 Now jump back to the middle climb the vine and pull out a (Deku Stick) light it and lit the unlit brazier to open the door.
